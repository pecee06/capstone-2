About Me

I am Priyanshu. So common of a name, I know. From my childhood till date, I find a colleague with same name and it creates confusion for others.

I cracked JEE in my first attempt and got Computer Science branch here at MANIT Bhopal. Right from 10th grade I was clear that whether I do B.Tech or not, I'm going to make my career in computers.

I have an enthusiasm of learning stuff like programming, development, etc. While I was in 11th grade, we had Python as our 1st programming language. Being simple syntactically, I convinced myself that other languages are of no use and Python is supreme.

I backed myself with data. Yes, I read on google that the most used programming language today is Python. Little did I know, that the reason was not the supiriority of Python over other languages but AI & ML.

Eventually I got to realize that for every purpose you have a certain language which would be its domain. Finally the quest to find the 'best' general purpose language came to a halt. Similar sort of quest was there in me between SQL and NoSQL.

I guess every learner at some point of time develops these quests which clears off gradually. And that's the fun of this journey, just like to understand your friends better, you got to spend time with them. Same goes for technology, to understand them, you need to spend time and stay loyal with it.
